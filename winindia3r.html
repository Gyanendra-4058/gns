"The greatest pleasure in LIFE is doing what people say you cannot do" - IIT Kanpur, YOU HAVE DONE IT!

"The greatest pleasure in LIFE is doing what people say you cannot do" - IIT Kanpur, YOU HAVE DONE IT!

What a response! AS the final results of Inter IIT challenge culminates, IIT Kanpur raced clear this time to finish at the top of the podium. All the other IITs were equally competitive - IIT Delhi and IIT Kharagpur deserves a special mention as they were neck to neck throughout the entire competition.

*However, as the going got tough (noting, the number of participants tripled with every passing round), IIT Kanpur again demonstrated what it is capable of - we performed exceedingly well to clinch the title. It was a breathtaking, fierce and extremely competitive affair until the very end - we never gave up and continued to pile on the points table until the last minute.

We might have just won a battle but the War is far from being over. War on COVID-19 continues and our challenges of being fit and healthy (Wellness Indoors- WIN) continues. The quest for fitness does not end here - please stay indoors, safe and healthy until these unprecedented time end.

A proud Secretary,
"The biggest source of motivation are your own thoughts, so think big and motivate yourself to win each and every challenge."
