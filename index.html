Our Events

Udghosh

Udghosh, the Annual Sports Festival of IIT Kanpur, is built on the same foundations and propagates the spirit of sports.It offers a platform for students from all over India to showcase their talent and compete with the best upcoming sports person in the country in a highly charged and competitive ambiance with state-of-the-art sports facilities

Visit Site

Josh

JOSH is IIT Kanpur's all night sports competition, rapidly becoming an annual fixture since its inception in 2004. JOSH invites each and every resident of IIT Kanpur to take part in more than twenty different games, all keenly contested with enthusiasm, energy and sportsmanship.


Visit Site

Inferno

The Inter Hall Sports Festival which provides a platform for bringing forward the sporting talent around the campus where students play hard to fight for the glory of their respective halls. Freshers Inferno is a subpart of this festival conducted to scout new talent and bring out the enthusiasm in freshmen.

Visit Site

Enthusia

Enthusia is the PG Sports League of IIT Kanpur. It is conducted in the even semester with the aim of encouraging PG participation in sports activities on campus. PG students from different halls are seen competing and cheering on various fields and courts. The enthusiasm, finesse and competition is a feast to the eyes
