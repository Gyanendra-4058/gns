Introduction

Athletics is a collection of sporting events that involve competitive running, jumping, throwing, and walking.
IIT Kanpur provides excellent facilities for Athletics enthusiasts with well-maintained grass track, throwing arena and facilities for vault, high Jumps and long jumps
