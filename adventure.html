Adventure Club

IIT Kanpur

Introduction

This life is all about having different experiences. One of such great experiences is the see the nature in its purest form where you will get the know about the bonding between you and the nature. Keeping this thing in mind, Adventure Sports Club at IIT Kanpur, established in 1992, is the forum for adventure enthusiasts, trekkers, climbers and armchair mountaineers in the student community. We work to develop the spirit of adventure in the campus. Adventure Sports Club provides you opportunity to roam in the mountains and to know about yourself and your own existence

Our Vision
The aim of the activities of our club is to inspire people to come out of their comfort zone and take part, enthusiastically, in various activities like trekking, cycling, climbing etc. We want our IITK junta to establish a close relation with Mother Nature (because thatâ€™s what we are part of and we must understand/appreciate it). We want to let them feel the serene and adventurous environment of the places where it is difficult to reach.

Our Activites

Trekking and Cycling

71 participants are going for the mentioned four treks: Kanchenjunga Base Camp Trek(KBC), Goechala Trek- Sikkim, Sandaphu Trek- West Bengal, Sarpass Trek Himachal Pradesh.Apart from this, there is a cycling trek also, along the Silk Route-Sikkim which itself is an achievement


Trekking and mountain biking

34 participants have successfully completed Kedarkantha Trek, Dodital Trek and Manali - Leh - Khardungla cycling which in itself is a historical achievement for participants to reach at Khardung La top by cycle.


Physical conditioning camp

It includes high intensity training which helps to prepare the trekkers to cope up with the adverse environment in the mountains. It also includes cross-country races like 5km, 8km and 10km to be completed by each individual willing to go for the treks.
