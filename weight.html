Introduction

Weightlifting, is a sport in which the athlete attempts a maximum-weight single lift of a barbell loaded with weight plates.The sport of weightlifting is muscle-building activity involving heavy weight. The two competition lifts in order are the snatch and the clean and jerk.

IIT Kanpur provides a full- fledged weightlifting hall with 5 platform along with a fully equipped gym
